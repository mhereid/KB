## Event Overview
The Mountains 2 Beach Marathon weekend takes place April 18-19, 2026, featuring multiple race distances: Marathon, Half Marathon, 10K, 5K, and Kids 1K.

## Critical Requirements for Packet Pickup
Participants must bring:
- Photo ID or copy of ID
- Digitally signed waiver email confirmation

**NO ID, NO WAIVER, NO PACKET!!!!**

## Packet Pickup Schedule
**Saturday, April 18:**
- 6:00-7:00 AM: 5K and Combo Challenge runners only
- 10:00 AM-5:00 PM: All other events (Full Marathon, Half Marathon, 10K, Kids 1K)

**Location:** San Buenaventura State Beach Park, 901 San Pedro St, Ventura, CA 93001

**Race Day Pickup** (Sunday, April 19) available only for those who pre-purchased this option at respective start lines, 4:30-5:30 AM.

## Start Line Locations & Times
All races begin at **6:10 AM Sunday, April 19**, except:
- **5K**: Saturday, 7:30 AM at State Beach
- **Kids 1K**: Saturday, 1:00 PM at State Beach

**Marathon start:** Ojai (Signal St & Hwy 150)
**Half Marathon start:** Oak View (Santa Ana Blvd)
**10K start:** Crooked Palm (N Ventura Ave)

## Shuttle Information
Free shuttles operate from two locations:
- Downtown Ventura (Plaza Park off Santa Clara St)
- Ventura County Government Center (800 S Victoria Rd)

**Critical reminder:** Arrive 30 minutes early; plan parking in advance with backup locations.

## Course Cutoffs
**Full Marathon:**
- Mile 6.4 by 8:05 AM
- Mile 12.4 by 9:45 AM
- Mile 22.5 by 11:30 AM

**Half Marathon:**
- Mile 10 by 11:30 AM (5.5 hours)

Past these points, course support is removed; runners receive maps and water to finish independently.

## Aid Stations
**Full Marathon:** 16 stations (A-O), averaging 1.5-2 miles apart
**Half Marathon:** 8 stations (H-O)
**10K:** 4 stations (L-O)
**5K:** 1 station at mile 1.4

All provide water and basic first aid; select stations offer electrolyte drinks or energy gels.

## Gear Check
Available at start lines (4:00-6:00 AM) for Marathon, Half Marathon, and 10K runners only. One standard grocery bag maximum; clearly mark with bib number. Neither Mountains 2 Beach nor any of its sponsors are responsible for lost, stolen or damaged bags/items.

## Awards
Awards are distributed immediately at the finish expo with no ceremony:
- **Marathon/Half:** Top 5 overall (male/female) + top 3 in each age group
- **10K/5K:** Top 3 overall + 1st in each age group

## Post-Race Activities
- **Finish Line Festival:** Free snacks, adult beverage garden (21+), food trucks
- **Shake Out Run:** Saturday, 9:30 AM with Mizuno demo shoes
- **Coffee with Race Director:** Saturday, 10:30-11:15 AM
- **After Party:** Sunday, 6:30 PM at Mother's Tacos (Downtown Ventura)

## Parking Notes
Parking is limited throughout the event. State Beach parking uses QR code payment system. Downtown Ventura offers ~2,000 free spaces. Plan ahead to avoid missing the race.

## Weather Preparation
Typical conditions: 55°F at start, 72°F at finish. Bring warm clothing for the start line and wear sunscreen.

## Important Policies
**Trash Zone Policy:** Discard waste only within designated trash zones (150 yards before/after aid stations). Littering may result in disqualification.

**No Spectators/Strollers on Shuttles:** Due to overcrowding, only athletes may board shuttles.

For additional information, visit the Mountains 2 Beach website or email info@mountains2beachmarathon.com.

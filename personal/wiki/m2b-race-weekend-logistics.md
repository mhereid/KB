### Saturday Evening — Pre-Marathon Dinner

Early dinner recommended (5:00–6:30 PM) so Mike can get to bed early. All of these are within 10 minutes of the Marriott and family-friendly:

1. **Cafe Fiore** — 66 S California St (Downtown). Italian trattoria with handmade pasta, fresh bread, and family-style platters. Ideal for carb-loading. Dinner from 5:00 PM. Reservations recommended: (805) 653-1266.

2. **Capriccio** — 298 E Main St (Downtown). Traditional Italian with a dedicated kids menu and gelato. Cozy corner bistro. Wed all-day happy hour.

3. **SPASSO Cucina Italiana** — 1140 S Seaward Ave. Roman-style pizza and pasta near the beach. Their lasagna gets top reviews. Higher-end but relaxed atmosphere.

4. **Brophy Bros.** — 1559 Spinnaker Dr (Ventura Harbor, closest to the Marriott). Seafood with harbor views. Not Italian, but has pasta options and is great for kids who want fish & chips. Walk-up wait can be long — arrive early or put your name in.

5. **Rumfish y Vino** — 4545 E Main St. Caribbean-inspired gastropub with an outdoor patio, fireplace, tacos, and ceviches. More adventurous menu but very kid-friendly atmosphere.

**Mike's pre-race nutrition note:** Stick to familiar carbs — pasta, bread, rice. Avoid high-fiber, spicy, or heavy/fatty foods. Hydrate well but don't overdo it.

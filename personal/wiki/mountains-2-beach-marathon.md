# Mountains 2 Beach Marathon

Mountains 2 Beach (M2B) is a point-to-point marathon weekend in Ventura County, California, held April 18-19, 2026, featuring marathon, half marathon, 10K, 5K, and kids 1K distances. The course runs from the mountains of Ojai down to the coast at San Buenaventura State Beach, with staggered start locations by distance, free shuttle service, and strict course cutoffs that enforce a roughly 5.5-hour finishing window. ## Start Locations

Each distance starts from a different point along the course:
- **Marathon** — Ojai (Signal St & Hwy 150)
- **Half Marathon** — Oak View (Santa Ana Blvd)
- **10K** — Crooked Palm (N Ventura Ave)
- **5K / Kids 1K** — San Buenaventura State Beach (Saturday)

## Course Cutoffs

The full marathon enforces three intermediate cutoffs:
- **Mile 6.4** by 8:05 AM
- **Mile 12.4** by 9:45 AM
- **Mile 22.5** by 11:30 AM

The half marathon cutoff is mile 10 by 11:30 AM (5.5 hours). After cutoff, course support is removed — runners receive maps and water to finish independently.

## Logistics

**Packet Pickup:** San Buenaventura State Beach Park, 901 San Pedro St, Ventura, CA 93001. Requires photo ID and digitally signed waiver confirmation.

**Shuttles:** Free from Downtown Ventura (Plaza Park off Santa Clara St) and Ventura County Government Center (800 S Victoria Rd). Arrive 30 minutes early. Athletes only — no spectators or strollers.

**Gear Check:** Available at start lines 4:00-6:00 AM for marathon, half, and 10K. One grocery bag maximum, marked with bib number.

**Parking:** Limited throughout. State Beach uses QR code payment. Downtown Ventura has ~2,000 free spaces.

## Aid Stations

- **Full Marathon:** 16 stations (A-O), every 1.5-2 miles
- **Half Marathon:** 8 stations (H-O)
- **10K:** 4 stations (L-O)
- **5K:** 1 station at mile 1.4

All stations provide water and basic first aid. Select stations offer electrolyte drinks and energy gels.

## Weather & Preparation

Typical conditions: 55°F at the start, warming to 72°F at the finish. Bring warm clothing for the start line and wear sunscreen.

**Trash Zone Policy:** Discard waste only within designated zones (150 yards before/after aid stations). Littering may result in disqualification.

## Awards

Distributed immediately at the finish expo — no ceremony:
- **Marathon/Half:** Top 5 overall (male/female) + top 3 per age group
- **10K/5K:** Top 3 overall + 1st per age group
